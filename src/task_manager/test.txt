＃ 從廚房冰箱拿牛奶，從客廳櫃子拿杯子，在廚房桌子1把牛奶倒進杯子裡，然後把倒好的杯子送到客廳桌子1，最後把牛奶放回廚房冰箱。
👉 Get the milk from the Kitchen Fridge and get the cup from the Living Room Cabinet. Pour the milk into the cup at Kitchen Table 1. Then bring the cup with milk to Living Room Table 1. Finally, put the milk bottle back into the Kitchen Fridge.

任務B：泡藥水準備
從廚房冰箱拿出藥水，從廚房桌子2拿出空杯子，在廚房桌子1把藥水倒入杯子，然後把杯子送到房間A書桌，再把藥水瓶放回廚房冰箱。
👉 Take the medicine bottle from the Kitchen Fridge and take the empty cup from Kitchen Table 2. Pour the medicine into the cup at Kitchen Table 1. Deliver the cup to Room A Desk. Then return the medicine bottle to the Kitchen Fridge.

任務C：準備點心盤
從廚房冰箱拿蘋果，從廚房桌子1拿盤子，在廚房桌子1把蘋果放到盤子上，再從客廳櫃子拿餐巾紙放到盤子旁邊，最後把整個盤子端到客廳桌子2。
👉 Get the apple from the Kitchen Fridge and get the plate from Kitchen Table 1. Place the apple onto the plate at Kitchen Table 1. Then get a napkin from the Living Room Cabinet and place it next to the plate. Finally, bring the plate with apple and napkin to Living Room Table 2.

任務D：換床頭書
從房間A書桌拿新書，把房間A床上的舊書拿走放到客廳桌子1，再把新書放到房間A床上，最後把客廳桌子2上的書籤放到新書裡（放在房間A床上）。
👉 Pick up the new book from Room A Desk. Take the old book from Room A Bed and put it on Living Room Table 1. Place the new book on Room A Bed. Then take the bookmark from Living Room Table 2 and place it with the new book on Room A Bed.

任務E：整理急救包
